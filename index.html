Welome!

This is the official unofficial page for Kempo Martial Arts, in Toledo, Ohio.

Kempo Martial Arts is a Dojo located in Toledo, Ohio. We want to spread the knowledge of our form, while also helping people become the person they want to be. We hope to help our students build Respect, both for themselves and for others. We also want to help them learn to Focus, whether it be focusing for an hour long class, or focusing on a long-term goal. Doing this will take a lot of Self Discipline, but with support from others that is something anyone can get. We believe that having Self Control and Balance are the key to sucess, not only in learning Martial arts, but in all of your life. While it's true that the things we teach could save you if your life was in danger, we hope to teach someting more than that. We hope to teach our students that there is nothing you cannot do, no problem you cannot overcome, if you have the proper mindset. If you Never Give Up you can do anything!
